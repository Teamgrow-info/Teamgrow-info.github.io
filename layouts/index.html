Case Study: Marches publics.com

$55,200 Annual Savings
65% Return on Investment

The Challenge

Marches publics.com needed to optimize their Google Cloud Platform costs while maintaining their service quality.

Our Solution

Through careful analysis and optimization of their cloud resources, we implemented:

  • Using spot instances to reduce the costs of the crawlers
  • Use a progressive switch to archive mode on files inside Google Cloud Storage
  • Transform Crawlers as jobs, to only create instances when needed

The Results

Our optimization strategies led to significant cost reduction while improving system efficiency:

  • Annual savings of $55,200
  • 65% return on investment
  • Maintained service performance (and even increasing stability via the use of jobs for crawlers)
